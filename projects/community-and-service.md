# Community & service

The part of the week that isn't measured in citations.

## What it is

Through **Chi Alpha**, I've led a small group of five or six people and helped organize service around Houston. The science is the loud part of my life; this is the quiet part that keeps it honest.

## A few of the things we've done

- Shoe cleanings for people who needed them
- Repairs and work for a local women's shelter
- Cooking and serving food for neighbors experiencing homelessness

> Mentoring and service run on the same engine: pay attention to a person, break the hard thing into smaller things, and stay encouraging while they carry it.

I think most "hard" ideas — in a lab or out of one — are just well-explained ideas waiting to happen. That belief started here.
