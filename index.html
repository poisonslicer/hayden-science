1The Premise

A place for long arcs — not a beige template.

∞

Built to grow

The site is intentionally easy to edit, so it can grow without a redesign every time life changes. Research output, books, films, essays, ideas — whatever is worth keeping in the open.

1

Unmistakably personal

Strong color, soft texture, and roomy typography over muted template minimalism. A clear identity now, with space for projects and milestones to get more detailed over time.

The thing it's not

A muted, interchangeable template you forget the second you close the tab. This site picks warmth, color, and a human voice instead. The reframe: a personal site shouldn't look like everyone else's — it should feel like a person.

3How It Should Feel

Warm, personal, and a little loud.

✓ Sounds like this site

  • Warm, colorful, open. Talks to you, like a person.
  • Alive over time: "long arcs — things measured, not just posted."
  • Strong color, soft texture, roomy type. Identity you remember.
  • Low-friction and durable: plain Markdown, easy to edit.

✗ Not this site

  • Muted template minimalism you forget instantly.
  • Corporate-portfolio voice: "Leveraging synergies."
  • Locked-down CMS that needs a redesign to change.
  • A wall of links with no point of view.
