3How It Should Feel

Warm, personal, and a little loud.

  • Warm, colorful, open. Talks to you, like a person.
  • Alive over time: "long arcs — things measured, not just posted."

✗ Not this site

  • Corporate-portfolio voice: "Leveraging synergies."
  • Locked-down CMS that needs a redesign to change.
  • A wall of links with no point of view.
4What It Keeps in the Open

Three things, one job: stay honest over time.

Stats
h-index, live from Scholarsyncs daily · keeps history
live ↗
Public stats. Citations and chess rating sync themselves; books and movies are tracked over time — plus the last book I read and last thing I watched.
Blog
Markdown in → post outdrop a .md file, it appears
Markdown-first writing. Posts stay portable and readable. Frontmatter + a file in content/posts, and it shows up automatically. Comments included.
Edit mode
type “hayden” 🔑hidden & password-gated
A secret door. No visible admin button by design. A hidden keystroke unlocks the editor for stats and posts — quiet on the outside, easy on the inside.
